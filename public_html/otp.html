Occupational Therapy Programs

Hand Therapy

Skilled therapy in rehabilitation of traumatic injuries and reconstruction of the hand. Custom splinting—dynamic and static splints, taping techniques, arthritic education, adaptive equipment recommendations and training.

Shoulder Rehabilitation

Rehab for repetitive work related injuries, athletic and surgical repair.

Athletic & Repetitive Injuries

Rehabilitation of injured athletes, conditioning, strengthening, cross training, cardiac exercises and aquatics programs. Transition to independent program.

Ergonomics Program

Evaluation and jobsite consultation, educational on-site prevention programs, equipment recommendations and training onsite by Occupational Therapist certified in Ergonomics.

 

Work Conditioning

Team planning with Physical and Occupational Therapy for return-to-work strengthening and successful return to pre-injury functional status.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specializing in neurological developmental rehab (NDT), skilled experience in Bobath techniques for functional recovery from stroke, head injury and traumatic injury.

Home Safety Evaluation

Home visit for safety recommendations for accessibility in the functional activities in the home: dressing, bathing, transfers, railings, laundry, adaptive equipment, wheelchair evaluation.
